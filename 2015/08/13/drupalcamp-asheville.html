Savas Labs giving two presentations at Drupalcamp Asheville

By Chris Russo

·

Aug 13, 2015


Drupal, Drupal 8, Event

Drupalcamp Asheville, we’re coming!

Savas Labs will be represented at Drupalcamp Asheville this year! Anne and Chris are both presenting. Anne will present on Drupal 8 theming, roughly based off her blog post about it, a topic with very little reliable information out there in the wild, and Chris is presenting on his “personal git workflow, for everyone,” which is also based off a blog post he wrote recently.

We’re excited to head to beautiful Asheville to teach, share and learn. We might even get a hike in and some craft beer while we’re at it.
