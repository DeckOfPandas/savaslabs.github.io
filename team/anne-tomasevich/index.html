    • Anne received a B.S. in textile engineering and worked in the clinical research industry for five years before becoming a web developer. At Savas Labs, Anne translates designs to code, builds Drupal themes, works to improve user experience, and enthusiastically lints her SCSS. She is particularly interested in CSS preprocessors, CSS3, responsive design, styleguide-driven development, and automated testing. When her laptop is closed, Anne enjoys cooking, reading, and playing music.
